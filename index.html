About the League

A Decade of Bragging Rights

The Grove Championship has been running since 2016 — a 10-season journey of bold draft decisions, questionable trades, waiver wire heroics, and heartbreaking playoff exits.

What started as a group of friends competing for bragging rights has grown into one of the most competitive fantasy football leagues around. Every September, 12 managers go to war. Only one lifts the trophy in January.

Head-to-head weekly matchups, full point-per-reception scoring, and a 7-team playoff format make every roster decision count from Week 1 through the championship.
